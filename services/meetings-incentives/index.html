Why it works

Incentives live or die on details you can only know from the ground.

Which venue photographs better than it performs. Which restaurant can actually seat sixty without splitting the group. Which week the city doubles its hotel rates because of a trade fair nobody mentioned. Our regional teams carry this knowledge into every proposal — it's the difference between a booking and a programme that works.

Meetings & incentives FAQ

Common questions from planners.

More general questions are answered on our full FAQ page.

How do you charge for meeting and incentive programmes?+

Every programme is priced against your brief — the group, the dates, the venues and the level of service you want. You receive one tailor-made proposal with clear costs before anything is committed.

Do you work with agencies as well as direct corporate clients?+

Yes — incentive houses, event agencies and corporate planners all use us as their local ground partner. We fit into your process, whether you need full delivery or just the local pieces you can't source from a distance.

What group sizes can you handle?+

From a board meeting for eight to conferences and incentive groups of several hundred. The service is the same: one named contact, local research, and a programme designed around your goal.

Can you arrange a site inspection before we commit?+

Yes — for larger programmes we can arrange inspection visits so you see the venues and hotels with our local team before you decide.

Have a brief? Let's get started.

Pick your destination and send us the goal, the group and the dates. We'll come back fast with a tailor-made plan.
