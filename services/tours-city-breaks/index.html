Tours & city breaks FAQ

Common questions from operators and group organisers.

More general questions are answered on our full FAQ page.

Do you work with tour operators as a local ground handler?+

Yes — tour operators and travel agencies are a core part of our client base. We research, price and operate the local programme under your brand's requirements, and our rates are built to leave room for yours.

Is there a minimum group size?+

No rigid minimum — the right setup depends on the itinerary. Tell us the group and the budget and we will advise honestly on what works, from small private tours to full-coach series.

Can you provide guides in our group's language?+

In most cities we can arrange licensed guides across the major European languages, subject to season and availability — tell us the language when you brief us and we build the itinerary around confirmed guides.

How does pricing work for tours?+

You receive one itemised, tailor-made proposal per itinerary — accommodation, transport, guiding, meals and entrances — priced to actually work for your customers. No off-the-shelf packages.
