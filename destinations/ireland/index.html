Where we work

Irish cities and regions we know inside out.

Capital · our base

Dublin

Georgian squares, the docklands conference scene, storied pubs and stadium events — where most Irish programmes begin.

The west

Galway

The gateway to Connemara and the Wild Atlantic Way — festivals, music and the coast at its most dramatic.

The south

Cork & Kinsale

Ireland's food capital and its prettiest harbour town — markets, sailing and long table dinners.

The kingdom

Killarney & Kerry

The Ring of Kerry, lakes and links golf — the classic incentive and touring heartland.

The north

Belfast & the Causeway Coast

Titanic heritage, the Giant's Causeway and a city reinventing itself — a compelling northern chapter for island itineraries.

Medieval Ireland

Kilkenny

Castle, crafts and cobbled lanes — an easy, atmospheric stop between Dublin and the south.

Ireland FAQ

Common questions about Irish programmes.

More general questions are answered on our full FAQ page.

Which Scancoming office handles trips to Ireland?+

Our Dublin office. One named contact briefs, plans and books your entire Irish programme — reachable out of hours as well.

Do you cover the whole island, including Northern Ireland?+

Yes — our Dublin team arranges programmes across the island of Ireland, from Cork and Kerry to Galway, and north to Belfast and the Causeway Coast.

What is the best season for an Irish programme?+

May to September gives the longest days and liveliest calendar, but Ireland works year-round — cosy winter programmes built around music, food and firesides have a charm of their own. We plan around the season, not against it.

Can you build golf into an incentive programme?+

Yes. Ireland's links courses are among the best in the world, and golf days combine naturally with touring and city elements in a single itinerary. Tell us the group's level and we will build around it.

Can an Irish trip be combined with the UK?+

Very naturally — Dublin and London are an hour apart by air, and our Ireland and UK offices work as one team. One brief covers the combined itinerary.

Planning something in Ireland?

Send the goal, the group and the dates to our Dublin team. We'll come back fast with a tailor-made plan.
