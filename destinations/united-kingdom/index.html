UK FAQ

Common questions about British programmes.

More general questions are answered on our full FAQ page.

Which Scancoming office handles trips to the UK?+

Our London office. One named contact briefs, plans and books your entire UK programme, backed by a nationwide supplier network — and reachable out of hours.

Do you arrange programmes outside London?+

Yes — Edinburgh, Manchester, Oxford, Cambridge, Bath and beyond. Our London team works with a supplier network across England, Scotland and Wales, so one brief can cover a multi-city UK itinerary.

Can you get West End theatre and Premier League tickets for groups?+

Tickets and events are one of our three core services. We source theatre, sport and concert tickets through long-standing local relationships, with seats and timings coordinated with the rest of your programme.

Do you handle transport and transfers within the UK?+

Yes — airport transfers, coaches, rail bookings and drivers are part of the programmes we build, planned so the logistics disappear into the background.

Can a UK trip be combined with Ireland?+

Very naturally — London and Dublin are an hour apart by air, and our UK and Ireland offices work as one team. One brief covers the combined itinerary.

Planning something in the UK?

Send the goal, the group and the dates to our London team. We'll come back fast with a tailor-made plan.
