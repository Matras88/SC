Where we work

Corners of the Grand Duchy we know inside out.

Nothing in Luxembourg is more than an hour away — a single programme can touch most of these.

Capital

Luxembourg City

A UNESCO-listed old town stacked over the Alzette gorge — historic venues above, the atmospheric Grund district below.

Business district

Kirchberg

The financial centre and European institutions, the Philharmonie and modern conference capacity — Luxembourg's corporate heart.

Wine country

The Moselle Valley

Crémant cellars and riverside vineyards twenty minutes from the capital — tastings, boat trips and long lunches.

Castle country

Vianden

One of Europe's great hilltop castles above a storybook town — a highlight for incentives and touring groups alike.

Little Switzerland

The Müllerthal

Rock canyons, forest trails and waterfalls — walking and team programmes an easy drive from the city.

Industrial heritage

Esch & Belval

Blast furnaces turned cultural quarter — a striking, unexpected venue setting for modern corporate events.

Luxembourg FAQ

Common questions about Luxembourg programmes.

More general questions are answered on our full FAQ page.

Which Scancoming office handles trips to Luxembourg?+

Our Benelux office in Brussels covers Luxembourg alongside Belgium and the Netherlands — one named contact plans and books the whole programme, reachable out of hours.

Is Luxembourg worth more than a day trip?+

Yes — the UNESCO-listed old town, the Moselle wine valley, castle country to the north and the Müllerthal hiking region fill a rewarding two- or three-day programme. It also pairs naturally with Belgium in a cross-border itinerary.

Can you arrange corporate meetings in the Kirchberg business district?+

Yes. Meetings and conferences on Kirchberg — home to Luxembourg's financial sector and European institutions — are a natural fit, with hotels, dinners and transport arranged around them.

What languages do suppliers in Luxembourg work in?+

Luxembourgish, French and German are all in daily use, and English is widespread in business settings. Our team handles supplier negotiations locally; you keep one English-speaking contact.

Can Luxembourg be combined with Belgium or the Netherlands?+

Easily. Luxembourg City is around three hours from Brussels by road or rail, and one office plans all three countries — so a combined itinerary needs only one brief and one contact.

Planning something in Luxembourg?

Send the goal, the group and the dates to our Benelux team. We'll come back fast with a tailor-made plan.
